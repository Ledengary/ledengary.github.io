Research Papers

Defeating Author Gender Identification with Text Style Transfer

R. Khan Mohammadi, S. A. Mirroshandel.(Under Review)
Pre-print version on arXiv

In this research project, We studied linguistical and sociolinguistical differences of male/female text based on their word choices and proposed a novel approach of style transfer in gender domain. An other interesting novelty of this research was the Multilingualism of our method, which unlike other approaches, is able to be applied on multiple languages. All in all, the outcome of this research was the proposal of PGST, a Polyglot Gender Style Transfer.


ParsLase: Towards better Abstractive Summarization in Persian

R. Khan Mohammadi, S. A. Mirroshandel.(In Preparation)

In this work, we gathered a large-scale Persian dataset for abstractive summarization and explored multiple approaches in this regard. During the process not only I have faced with the difficulties of applying some of the most popular abstractive summarization techniques, but also we are working on a brand new method of summarization for obtaining better results in Persian to end up publishing it as a research paper.


ParsCap: Investigating Bilingual Captions with Multi-task Learning

R. Khan Mohammadi, S. A. Mirroshandel.(In Preparation)

In this research project We are gathering a Persian Image Captioning dataset and investigating different effects of Multi-task Learning to generate better Persian captions.


ParsHealth: A Persian Knowledge Graph Based Question Answering platform in Healthcare domain

R. Khan Mohammadi, M. Allahyari.(In Preparation)

In this research project We gathered a large-scale Persian Healthcare dataset and constructed a knowledge Graph for Question Answering purposes. In this work, we are facing multiple challenges, such as Relation Extraction in Persian and Graph-To-Text algorithms over a knowledge graph in healthcare domain.
